We Are A Team Come Join Us

If you are a conscientious individual who wants seasonal work and enjoys travel - in an atmosphere of mutual respect for the employer, employee and the customer, we have a job for you!

Job Description

We are looking for candidates who have at least six months farm experience and/or truck driving experience. Also, other related experience or training such as welding, machinery or truck maintenance and repair, and engineering is a plus.

Requirements

  • Individuals must have a clean driving record and a stable work history
  • All employees are required to have a current US license
  • Employees must pass a physical exam and pre-employment drug screen by company selected physician
  • Operator/drivers are expected to perform required daily maintenance and field repairs when needed
  • cooperative attitude and a willingness to work with others in a team environment is a must
  • Candidates must speak, read, and write English. Consulate interviews are required in your respective countries. Criminal and driving history will be examined
  • Foreign workers are employed when US workers are not available. H2A visas are available and qualified workers should apply no later than March 1st each year

    • Employee Compensation

    The US Department of Labor sets our minimum wage rates and these rates differ by state and are adjusted periodically. Current rates are available upon request. Employees are paid bi-weekly. We guarantee a minimum of $2300/mo U.S. average for the season and greater potential earnings possible dependent on hours worked, safety, and performance.
