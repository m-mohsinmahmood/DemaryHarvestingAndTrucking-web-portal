About Us

Demaray Harvesting and Trucking, LLC (“DHT”) was established in 1974 by Warren and SeDelle Demaray near Jamestown ND. Their son, Tim, joined in 1977, eventually taking over and building the company into an industry leader.

Today, the company operates a large fleet of combines and trucks country wide.

Today DHT has two primary regional operating centers, one in Casa Grande, AZ and the other in Aberdeen, South Dakota.
