Our Services

DHT takes great pride in providing a full range of custom agricultural services such as harvesting, farming, trucking and combine/tractor rentals country wide. Our years of industry experience and expertise also allow us to harvest a wide variety of crops with confidence.

1

Custom Harvesting

DHT offers a variety of custom harvesting solutions that range from full outsourcing to partial outsourcing (when the landowner prefers to utilize some of his/her equipment). Our harvesting equipment is state-of-the-art, well-maintained, and high-capacity to ensure efficiency, quality, and on-time performance.

Combine/Tractor Rentals

DHT also offers landowners an ability to rent one or more of our newer CASE model combines including delivery & pick-up as well as DHT operators, if applicable.

Custom Farming

DHT has been custom farming since 2000 and offers landowners a wide variety of custom farming solutions which includes labor, insurance, and the required equipment to offer a wide range of services such as ripping, swathing, seeding, tilling, etc. DHT also provides flexible contract structures depending on the needs of the customer.

Commercial Trucking

DHT can meet your agricultural (and other) transportation needs. We have a permanent staff of drivers who have pulled hopper bottoms, walking floors, drop decks, and machinery haulers transporting apples, potatoes, cotton seed, fertilizer, milo, oats, sunflowers, wheat, barley, beans, silage, hay, palletized wheat seed, ag machinery, etc. We operate a variety of trucks and trailers to move your valued cargo safely and efficiently.
